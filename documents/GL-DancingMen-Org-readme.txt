- About GL-DancingMen-Org font
  GL-DancingMen-Org is based on the Cipher from "The Adventure of the Dancing Men", The Return of Sherlock Holmes by Arthur Conan Doyle.
  We already made GL-DancingMen, but for the 10th anniversary of this font, we made one more cipher font from original illustration.

  The Font includes only "A" to "Z", "a" to "z" and numerals.
  Alphabet "F", "J", "K", "Q", "U", "W", "X", "Z" and numerals are the same as GL-DancingMen.

  We have made all CHARACTERS appeard in original work into font data. So some characters have alternate glyphs.

  In this font, 7 discretionary ligatures (dlig) are prepared.
  - aM herE abE slaney
  - aT elriges
  - comE elsie
  - never
  - elsiE preparE tO
  - meeT thY god
  - comE herE aT oncE
